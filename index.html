FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CUET preparation and CUETAce.

What is a CUET mock test? +
A CUET mock test is a full-length practice exam that replicates the actual NTA CUET CBT interface — with the same timer, question palette colours, and +5/−1 marking scheme. CUETAce mock tests have 50 questions per subject, matching the actual CUET exam pattern. Each test is followed by detailed results, SWOT analysis, and chapter-wise performance breakdown.
How to prepare for CUET Commerce 2026? +
The most effective CUET Commerce preparation strategy is: (1) Complete your NCERT Class 12 syllabus first. (2) Practice chapter-wise on CUETAce to identify weak areas. (3) Attempt full subject-wise mock tests under timed conditions. (4) Review your SWOT analysis after each test and revisit weak chapters. (5) Attempt past year papers (2022–2025) in the last 30 days before the exam. Consistency of 2–3 hours daily for 3 months is sufficient for most Commerce students.
Is CUETAce free? What's included in the free plan? +
Yes — CUETAce is free to start with no registration required. The free plan includes 3 full mock tests (60 questions each) for any subject. After the free tests, you can upgrade: Basic plan (₹100 one-time) gives unlimited mock tests and 30-question chapter-wise practice. Premium plan (₹200 one-time) gives everything — 200 questions per chapter, past year papers in attempt mode, and detailed explanations for every question.
Which subjects are covered for CUET Commerce? +
CUETAce covers all 5 Commerce stream subjects for CUET 2026: Accountancy (16 chapters, 1,800+ questions), Business Studies (12 chapters, 1,600+ questions), Economics (15 chapters, 1,900+ questions), General Test (9 chapters, 1,500+ questions), and English (5 chapters, 1,200+ questions).
What is the CUET marking scheme? +
The official NTA CUET marking scheme is +5 marks for every correct answer and −1 mark for every wrong answer. Unattempted questions carry 0 marks. CUETAce mock tests use this exact marking scheme so your practice scores accurately reflect your expected CUET performance.
Are CUET past year papers available on CUETAce? +
Yes — CUETAce Premium includes official CUET past year papers from 2022 to 2025 for all 6 Commerce subjects. Premium users can attempt these papers in the full NTA CBT exam mode with timer and marking, and review answers with detailed explanations afterwards.
